About this Website

I created this website as a supplement to my resume. Not only does it show a more detailed look at my previous work, but I also coded this entire (albeit static) website from scratch. It's an original design to show off my versatility. I try to focus on a simple, but attractive design. Like many modern design trends, I prefer flat, material graphics with few colors and big bold images. The colors chosen for this website follow a theme, as all good things do.

Astronauts Blue: #81b1c9

Astronomers Green: #e8f2eb

Cosmic Latte: #FFF8E7

Cosmic Latte is the average color of all the stars of the universe, and I definetly shoehorned it in.

I do love to use libraries, they speed up development and are extensively tested for security. Libraries are half the reason I favor Python for my personal projects. For a simple website like this, I only used Bootstrap, FontAwesome, and jQuery.
